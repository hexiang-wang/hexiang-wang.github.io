Welcome!

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the School of Civil Engineering, Tianjin University, China. I received my doctoral degree in civil engineering from the University at Buffalo on February 2018, followed with a one-year postdoc in University of California, Davis.

My research interests are stochastic finite element analysis, structural risk and reliability, soil structure interaction, etc. Broadly speaking, I am generally interested in numerical algorithms, large-scale computing problems, programming, applied mathematics, etc. Therefore, some useful computational tools and scripts are provided and useful experiences are documented for interested readers. For more information, please visit here.
